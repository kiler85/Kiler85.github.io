1 - FARM | BUILD

30 min

➜ There is a period of farming, building and crafting for 30 minutes after the start of the competition.

HOW IT WORKS

Several teams each composed of several players find themselves on a map where they will have to build a base, prepare equipment and what to raid the bases of other players, To eliminate a team they must break their tc. The winner will be the team that will accumulate the most points at the end of the 2h30, you can win even if you have been eliminated.
